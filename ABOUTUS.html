About Us

Our Story and What We Stand For

The Shangri-La group is known for its numerous hotels, restaurants, and resorts but is also more than noteworthy in discussions of real-estate and sustainable growth Our headquarters is located in Hong Kong SAR and serve as pioneers for Asian Hospitality. There are more than 100 hotels worldwide under the Shangri-La group with over 30,000 rooms in total. Under the group are other hotel brands: Kerry Hotels, JEN, and Traders.

The name was derived from James Hilton's The Lost Paradise which described in its story a safe haven in an inhospitable world. Today, the company embodies this concept by continuing to serve customers with humility and respect.

We at Shangri-La offer you only the best of the best. We are here to deliver the deluxe treatment to all of our guests and provide and greater than pleasant experience to all. Not only do we promise this treatment to our guests but also to those who are to partner and work with us.
