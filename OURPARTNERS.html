Our Partners

Shangri-La group has a vast range of partners from across the globe. Our connections across different industries and cultures allow us to operate with nuance and professionalism. We work with our partners and investors to serve our clients to the best of our abilities.

CMBC

With China Minsheng Bank Credit Card Center’s philosophy of “customer, market and innovation orientation” at the core of the business, the bank has issued over 100 different credit card products. China Minsheng Bank is one of the leading credit card brands in a highly competitive market, providing cardholders with excellent customer service.

  

ICBC

ICBC is the largest custodian bank in China. Being the flagship of ICBC Group outside PRC, ICBC (Asia) provides quality services to fund managers, financial institutions and institutional investors. It is ICBC's goal to establish long term partnership with thier clients.

  

Great World Serviced Apartments

Located just 10 minutes away from the Central Business District and Orchard Road, Great World Serviced Apartments offers a wide range of accommodation options for everyone, including families with kids and pets. Stay from 6 nights to a year, there is an option to suit your needs.

  

KRISFLYER

KrisFlyer, the rewards programme of the Singapore Airlines Group, lets you earn miles whenever you fly on Singapore Airlines, Scoot, or partner airlines worldwide, or spend with over 200 partners on the ground. Redeem your miles for flight tickets, upgrades and many other rewards.

  


Corporate Profile

Shangri-La Asia Limited is our investment holding company and the principal activities are in ownership, development, operations and management of hotel properties, investment properties such as office / retail spaces, as well as residential developments for sale.

From our first hotel in Singapore in 1971, we have built deep expertise in Asian luxury hospitality across Asia, especially in China, and in many other parts of the world.

We have since grown to a diverse portfolio of businesses covering over 100 hotels and resorts, several mixed-use developments including retail and commercial real estate, over 500 food and beverage outlets, wellness and fitness facilities, and family-centric spaces.

  

 

Shangri-La Asia Limited

We operate our businesses under our multi-brands encompassing key areas in hotels and resorts, commercial and residential properties, food and beverage, and recreational experiences; including but not limited to “Shangri-La”, “Kerry Hotels”, “JEN by Shangri-La”, “Traders”, “Rasa”, “Summer Palace”, “Shang Palace”, “CHI, The Spa at Shangri-La”.

Incorporated in Bermuda with limited liability, our shares are primarily listed on The Stock Exchange of Hong Kong Limited (stock code 00069) with a secondary listing on the Singapore Exchange Securities Trading Limited (stock code S07) and US OTC (over the counter) traded ADR (stock code SHALY).
